Bridging the Past
& the Future

As one of the world's most dynamic metropolises, Shanghai carries not only China's ambition for the future but also the warmth of the first morning light hitting Shikumen gates. It is a battlefield of capital and a cradle for art.

Here, you can jog along the Bund at dawn, enjoy authentic Shengjian for lunch in a local eatery, and overlook the world's most dazzling skyline from a Lujiazui skyscraper at night. This extreme contrast forms the unique "Soul of Shanghai."
